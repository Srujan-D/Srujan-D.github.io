About Me

I am a pre-final year undergraduate student at the Computer Science Department at BITS Pilani, Pilani Campus, deeply interested in robotics. I am also exploring new areas related to deep learning, with an aim to eventually study about reinforcement learning.

I am currently affiliated with CSIR-CEERI Pilani working on indoor UAV project under the supervision of Mr Kaushal Kishore. I am also working on a ML based person state classification project at CEERI Chennai under the mentorship of Dr Madan Kumar Lakshmanan. I also spent my summer with the A.R.M.S. Lab at IIT Bombay supervised by Prof. Arpita Sinha, exploring the navigation stack for a tractor trailer robotic system.
